QSAR Prediction Enhancement :

Our project overcomes classical QSAR limitations by representing molecules as information-rich graphs. We then deploy a precision GNN-XGBoost ensemble with attention models to maximize predictive power. This strategy yielded a significant 15% improvement in accuracy over traditional baseline methods. Critically, our model offers enhanced interpretability, using visual heatmaps to pinpoint drivers of biological activity. This provides actionable insights for chemists, transforming the model from a black box into a valuable discovery tool.

  • TensorFlow / Keras
  • AIAgentic
  • Psi4
  • Streamlit

ADMET Toxicology Prediction :

To address the critical challenge of inaccurate ADMET prediction in early-stage drug discovery, we developed a precision machine learning model using realistic 2D and 3D molecular representations. Our ensemble approach—combining GNNs, Random Forest, and XGBoost with key chemical descriptors—achieved a 15% improvement in predicting major toxicities like hepatotoxicity and cardiotoxicity. Crucially, our model provides explainable outputs, using heatmaps to identify the specific molecular substructures that drive toxicity.
